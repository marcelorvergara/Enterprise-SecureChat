Proposal

Possibility of including BAR questions/answers, with restricted access to Reserves Coordinations and Managements. Reservoir teams would not be able to access this content.
Possibility for Business Unit users to insert context with access restrictions in Enterprise SecureChat. For example: Insert a business unit historical reserve volumes, but the content remains accessible only to this business unit Reserves Management.
Access: only sectorial reserves managements and reserves coordinations.
Content restriction:
ANP Questioning may contain information that must remain restricted;
Answer may contain information that must remain restricted;
Corporate answers can be accessed by all Business Units respecting roles and restricted points.

Points classified as restricted:

Reserves Volumes
End of Contract
Economic Limits
% Reserves Variations
Recovery Factor
Investment Years
Investment Values
Field Contract Information (Transfer of Rights Data / Dados Cessão Onerosa)
Barrel and sales gas prices
Document dates
Official Letter, Letter, and Process Number
